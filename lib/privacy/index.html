How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your My Connect account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Send an email or call us using the contact information below
  2. Provide Your Details: Include your registered mobile number and username
  3. Verification: We will verify your identity before processing the deletion
  4. Confirmation: You will receive confirmation once your account is deleted

Contact Information

Email: admin@myconnect.com

Phone: +91 9884559988

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your profile information (name, mobile, email, addresses)
  • Family information (father, grandfather names, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Profile photos and images
  • Work/business details
  • Education information
  • Chat messages (may be retained for up to 30 days for technical purposes)
  • My List data
  • Event and blog participation data

Data Retention Period

After receiving your deletion request:

  • Account deletion will be processed within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained for an additional 30 days for backup and recovery purposes
  • We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You will lose access to all app features immediately upon deletion
  • If you have pending transactions or active subscriptions, please resolve them before deletion
