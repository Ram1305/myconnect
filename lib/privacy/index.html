Privacy Policy – My Connect

Last updated: February 14, 2025

My Connect ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our My Connect mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: Name, mobile number, email address, profile photo
  • Family information: Father’s name, grandfather’s name, and other family details you choose to share
  • Location data: Address and location information you provide or that we obtain with your permission
  • Work and education: Business details, work history, education information
  • My List and events: Items you save and events you participate in

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data: App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Location data: GPS and approximate location when you grant permission (e.g., for maps, events)

1.3 Third-Party Services

We use services that may collect their own data, including:

  • Firebase: Analytics, crash reporting, and push notifications
  • Google Maps: Maps and location features
  • Image picker: Photos you select to upload

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create, manage, and secure your account
  • Provide app features such as profiles, chat, events, and maps
  • Send push notifications and in-app updates
  • Improve our services, fix bugs, and personalize your experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information:

  • With other users: As needed for features you use (e.g., profiles, chat, events)
  • With service providers: To support hosting, analytics, notifications, and similar services
  • For legal reasons: When required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Data is stored on secure servers and transmitted using encryption where applicable. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you use the app at your own risk.

5. Data Retention

  • We retain your account and profile data while your account is active
  • Chat messages may be kept for up to 30 days for technical purposes
  • After account deletion, some data may be retained for backups for up to 30 additional days
  • We may retain certain data where required by law or for legitimate business purposes

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and update: You can view and edit your profile and account details in the app
  • Location permissions: You can change location permissions in your device settings
  • Push notifications: You can disable notifications in app or device settings
  • Account deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us

For account deletion requests, see our Delete Account page.

7. Children's Privacy

My Connect is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the app or by other reasonable means. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
